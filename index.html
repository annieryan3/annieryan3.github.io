Annie Ryan

Hi! I'm Annie, an aspiring journalist and a student at The University of Maryland, College Park. I am planning to obtain my undergraduate degree in just three years.

I am currently studying journalism, with a minor in general business.

During my last two years at UMD, I have been apart of multiple campus publications, including Her Campus Maryland, Testudo Times and WMUC Sports. I also am currently an editorial intern at Modern Luxury Media, where I work on their Boston, Philadelphia, and D.C. publications. I am also a member of the Delta Delta Delta sorority at the Alpha Pi chapter of the University of Maryland, where I am on the philanthropy, sisterhood, merchandise and marketing committees.

I am originally from Damascus, MD, and in my free time I like to do hot yoga, go on walks, go shopping, and spend time with people I love.
